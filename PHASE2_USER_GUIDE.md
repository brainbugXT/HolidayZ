## New Analytics Features

### 1. Viewing the Savings Velocity Chart

**What it shows:**
- A line chart displaying monthly savings trends for the last 6 months
- Each family member has their own colored line
- Trend indicator shows if savings are increasing or decreasing

**How to access:**
1. Navigate to the Dashboard
2. Scroll to the "Analytics & Insights" section
3. The chart is displayed in the left panel

**How to read it:**
- Each point on the chart represents total savings for that month
- Hover over any point to see exact amounts
- Lines going up = increasing savings momentum
- Lines going down = decreasing savings momentum
- No data points = no contributions that month

### 3. Understanding Your Stats

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How is the monthly average calculated?**  
A: It's your total savings divided by the number of months you've contributed (not total months since joining).

**Q: What if I miss a month?**  
A: Your streak resets to 0, but your other stats remain unchanged. Start a new streak next month!

**Q: Can I see historical data beyond 6 months?**  
A: Currently the velocity chart shows the last 6 months. More historical views coming in future updates!

**Q: How often do stats update?**  
A: Stats update in real-time whenever a new contribution is made.

**Q: Can other family members see my stats?**  
A: Yes, any family member can click on your card in the Family Performance panel to view your stats. This encourages transparency and motivation!

**Q: What happens to my stats if I delete a contribution?**  
A: All stats automatically recalculate, so your totals, averages, and streak will update accordingly.
